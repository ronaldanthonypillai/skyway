Lesson Objectives

At the end of this lesson,

  • Student should have been given all necessary paperwork
  • Student should be introduced to all driving instructors they will be trained by as well as the Head of Safety and Compliance
  • New driver responsibilities and expectations should be discussed as well as the training plan outlined
  • Facility walkaround, including all pertinent areas to the job function, introduction to dispatch and dispatch procedures, as well as where supplies pertaining to all job functions can be found in corresponding areas.

Preparation

Instructor should have the classroom ready.

Materials Required

  • Policy Manual for handout
  • Acknowledgement form for Policy handout
  • Test questions available for handout

Teaching Notes

Daily Vehicle Inspection (“DVI”)

  • Instructor provide examples of application of policy during delivery
  • Engage participants to ensure understanding of Defensive Driving
  • Explain definition of Preventable accident, and at fault accident

Participant

  • Should follow along appropriately, and ask questions suitable to the material, particularly anything that couldn’t be shown in the classroom that would be better shown on the vehicle
